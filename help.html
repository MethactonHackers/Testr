Hello and welcome to Testr, your one stop for all of your test making needs. So to kick off the help section we will start off with on FAQ.

1.
Q: What types of tests do you support?
A: We currently support multible choice quiestions and short answer but in the future we plan on expanding.

2.
Q: How do I actully make a test? 
A: Simply go here and start using the simple to use UI.

3.
Q: How do I make a question?
A: Click on the big blue + button and a new question field will apear along with slots for possible answers.

4.
Q: How do I create possible answers?
A: Under the question you should see a slightly smaller big blue + button click than and it will allow you to add a new answer choice.

5.
Q: How do I delete a question if i]I do not want it?
A: Simply click the red X at the top right of the webpage.

6.
Q: How do I delete an answer choice?
A: Delete all of the text in the answer choice and then press backspace one more time and the text field should disappear.
7.

Q: How do I select the correct answer?
A: Use the drop down at the bottom of the question so select what number answer is the correct one.
